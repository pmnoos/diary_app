🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last updated: August 6, 2025

📋 Introduction

At Digital Diary App, we believe your thoughts, memories, and personal reflections should remain completely private. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our journaling application.

🔐 Privacy Promise: We will never read, sell, or share your journal entries. Your personal thoughts are encrypted and accessible only to you.

📊 Information We Collect

🔹 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password (encrypted)
  • Journal Content: Your diary entries, photos, and attachments (end-to-end encrypted)
  • Profile Settings: Display preferences, themes, and notification settings
  • Support Communications: Messages you send to our support team

🔹 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs (for security and debugging)
  • Analytics: App performance metrics (no personal content included)

⚠️ Important: We never collect the actual content of your journal entries for analysis or marketing purposes.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

✅ What We Do

  • Provide and maintain the journaling service
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Send important security notifications
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

❌ What We Never Do

  • Read or analyze your journal content
  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use your content for marketing
  • Track you across other websites
  • Access your data without permission

🛡️ How We Protect Your Data

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Your journal entries are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers. We cannot decrypt or read your content.

🏢 Secure Servers

Data is stored on encrypted servers with enterprise-grade security measures and regular security audits.

🔑 Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We use a zero-knowledge system - even our staff cannot access your encrypted journal content.

🔄 Regular Backups

Your encrypted data is automatically backed up to prevent data loss, while maintaining privacy protection.

🍪 Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Remember your app preferences
  • Ensure proper app functionality

Analytics Cookies

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand:

  • Which features are most popular (no personal data)
  • App performance and error rates
  • General usage patterns for improvements

🎛️ Your Control: You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings without affecting core app functionality.

🤝 Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Only Share Data When:

  • Required by Law: Court orders, legal processes, or government requests
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate (under strict privacy agreements)
  • Security Threats: To prevent fraud, abuse, or protect user safety
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

🚫 Never Shared: Your journal content is NEVER shared with third parties for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

Third-Party Services

🔧 Infrastructure Partners

  • Cloud hosting (encrypted data only)
  • Email delivery services
  • Payment processing (for premium features)

📊 Analytics Partners

  • Privacy-focused analytics (no personal data)
  • Crash reporting (anonymized)
  • Performance monitoring

🔍 Access & Portability

  • Download all your data anytime
  • Export in multiple formats
  • View what data we have about you

✏️ Correction & Control

  • Update your account information
  • Modify privacy settings
  • Control data sharing preferences

🗑️ Deletion Rights

  • Delete individual journal entries
  • Close your account completely

  • Disable analytics tracking
  • Turn off email notifications
  • Limit data processing

👶 Children's Privacy

Digital Diary App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

🔞 Age Requirement: Users must be 13 years or older to create an account. Users under 18 should have parental consent.

🌍 International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and use appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by data protection authorities
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct
